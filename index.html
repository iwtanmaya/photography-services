About Me

IW TANMAYA is a photography studio based in Odisha, India. It was founded in 2019 by Tanmaya Rout, a professional photographer with over 4 years of experience. IW TANMAYA specializes in wedding photography, portrait photography, and commercial photography. The studio has a team of experienced photographers and videographers who are passionate about capturing beautiful moments and creating memories that last a lifetime.
